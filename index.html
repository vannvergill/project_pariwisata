Explore the Region

Pesisir Barat

Home to Krui, this coastal regency offers some of the best surfing spots in the world, stunning sunsets, and pristine white sand beaches. A perfect blend of local culture and thrilling adventures.

Discover More

Way Kambas National Park

A designated ASEAN Heritage Park, renowned for its incredible biodiversity. Spot Sumatran elephants, rhinos, and tigers in their natural habitat, offering a truly magical wildlife encounter.

Practical Information

Transportation

Easily accessible via Radin Inten II International Airport (TKG) or by ferry from Merak (Java) to Bakauheni port.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from May to September offers the best weather for island hopping, surfing, and wildlife spotting.

Culinary

Don't miss local delicacies like Seruit, Engkak, and the world-famous Lampung Robusta coffee.
